Our Mission

Her Startr Foundation aims to empower women entrepreneurs to build successful businesses.Her Startr Foundation breaks down barriers for women to build successful businesses so that we may effectively improve the percentage of women building profitable, sustainable million-dollar-plus businesses. Our virtual network, local events and global mission is focused on the success of women entrepreneurs.
